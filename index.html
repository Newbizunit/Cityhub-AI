Process

From raw data to city action
in four steps

  1. 01

    Connect Your Data

    Plug in existing sensors, SaaS tools, and legacy systems via 200+ pre-built connectors. Zero coding required for standard integrations.

  2. 02

    AI Models Train on Your City

    Our platform fine-tunes models on your specific geography, population, and policies to maximise local accuracy.

  3. 03

    Insights Surface Automatically

    Anomalies, opportunities, and risks appear on your dashboard before they become problems—with plain-language explanations.

  4. 04

    Act with Confidence

    Approve AI-suggested actions or let automations run. Full audit trail kept for compliance and public accountability.
